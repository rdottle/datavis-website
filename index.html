BY RACHAEL CLAIRE DOTTLE

Hi. My name is Rachael, and I am a graduate assistant at the Empirical Reasoning Center at Barnard College and a fellow in the Recovery and Resiliency Group of New York City Transit, MTA . I do research at the intersection of data, GIS, urbanism, architecture and the humanities. I've previously worked as Data Science for Social Good Fellow at the University of Washington . This page is the main landing page for work developed in the Data Visualization for Architecture, Urbanism and the Humanities , taught by Juan Francisco Saldarriaga at the Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation of Columbia University .
